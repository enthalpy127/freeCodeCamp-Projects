What is watch? How it works?

Watch took center stage this year's Product Show with a preview of watch OS 2.0 . The next generation of Watch software is packed with features that you just might turn the device from a nice-to-have into a must-have. If you already own an Apple Watch, you'll have to live with the old software until fail. You'll love it at the first use.

How the watch is made?

Watch has an amaze aluminium case (which is lighter than stainless steel) in silver or space gray and the face is on ion-X glass, which also designed to be hard and rugged (as well as a little bit lighter) than the sapphire. It's definitely the lightest of three Apple Watch editors, making it on idal exercise companion.

Can I swap out the watch band?

Absolutely, Watch comes with six bands that are easy to mix and match any band with any watch. You can swap them out as your heart desires without the aid of any tools- as long as the band and the watch are the same size (The Leather loop band for example only fits 42nm watches, not the smaller 38nm size)

So it's a watch and a fitness tracker?

The accessories let the watch count your steps and extrapolate distance on its own or rely on the GPS in the paired phone to trace your exact route. That step data comes in handy for two of the apps included in the watch. Activity and Workout. Each of the watch's fitness apps sync data back to the Health and Fitness apps on your phone.
